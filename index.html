Everything you need to know about RenoAI and how it can transform your business.

How quickly will I start seeing results?

Most clients see their first qualified leads within 48-72 hours of implementation. However, the real magic happens over the first 30 days as our AI system learns your business, market, and ideal customer profile. The system continues to improve and optimize month after month, with many clients seeing 10x+ improvements within 90 days.

What if I already have a CRM or marketing tools?

Perfect! RenoAI integrates seamlessly with virtually all major CRM platforms including HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Monday.com, and many others. We also integrate with email marketing platforms, sales tools, and business automation software. Our system automatically syncs leads and data in real-time, eliminating manual data entry and ensuring your entire tech stack works together harmoniously. During your free strategy call, we'll map out your existing systems and create a custom integration plan that maximizes efficiency.

Is this only for large companies or does it work for small businesses?

RenoAI is specifically designed for small to mid-sized businesses. In fact, our sweet spot is companies with 1-50 employees who want to scale aggressively without massive marketing budgets. Small business owners often see the highest ROI because they're not weighed down by legacy systems and bureaucracy. We've helped solopreneurs, local service providers, real estate agents, and growing companies all achieve remarkable results. The system scales with your business—start small and expand as you grow.

What happens if the leads aren't a good fit for my business?

This is exactly why we have a comprehensive discovery and strategy phase before launch. We spend time understanding your ideal customer profile, your service area, your pricing, and your conversion process. Our AI learns from this information to only target prospects that are genuinely a good fit. Additionally, we provide feedback mechanisms so you can rate leads, and our system continuously learns and adjusts. If a lead type isn't converting, we pivot. We're constantly optimizing to ensure you're getting high-quality, relevant prospects that your sales team actually wants to work with.

Pricing varies based on your industry, market, and lead volume goals. Most clients see a 5-10x ROI within the first 90 days, making the investment incredibly cost-effective. We offer a 30-day trial period so you can experience the system before committing long-term.
